Spectators start in seagull mode.

Comm Center
Enables map unit markers and unit camera. Uprades can only be initiated with a CC. To be able to buy units remotely (Options\Buy Units) from a factory a CC must be near that factory.

Score is achieved by a group member killing infantry, damaging vehicles, damaging structures and taking towns.
The number of points received are based on the targets value.
For every point a cash bonus is received. This cash bonus is dealt in one minute intervals.

Back

The Options dialog is by default accessed by an action on the player when on foot and on the vehicle when mounted.

$ You/Side
Shows your and your side current cash.

Score(Pos)
Your current score and leaderboard position.

Leaderboard
Open the leaderboard dialog showing all groups current score.

Towns
Shows the currently number of controlled towns by your side and the total number of towns.

Cleanup Ground
This command will delete most ammo, weapons, destroyed vehicles and corpses near you.

Income
Shows the current town income based on the towns under your sides control.

Disband Unit
Here you can disband one of your units.

Transfer Money
Here you can give money to another player group or to the AI commander.

Buy Units
Open the Buy Units dialog.
At least one working Comm Center must exist to be able to open the dialog.
You will only be able to buy units from factories which are near a working Comm Center.

Statistics
Open the Statistics dialog showing all the number of built units and structures.
Statistics of the enemy will show at game over.

Unit Cam
Enter unit cam of the last tracked unit.
Unit cam is only available with a working Comm Center.

Sat Cam
Enter Satellite camera view.
Sat Cam is only available if upgraded and a working Comm Center exist.

Options by Action/Map Click
Setting to control if the Options dialog is accessed by the default action or by a click on the map with no selected units.

Extra AI Markers
Setting to control of which groups member markers (yellow dots) are shown.
All : show all groups members
Group : show only this group members

Player Income
Sets the percentage of town income that non-commander players will share.
Only the Commander can change this setting.
Transfer Command
The commander can give command to another player.

Command AI
Open the Command AI groups dialog.
The Command AI groups dialog can only be used by the Commander.

AI Respawn Pos
Sets/shows the current respawn location of AI group leaders.
Only the Commander can change this setting.

Upgrades
Shows available upgrades and their status: yellow: upgrade in progress
green: upgraded
Only the Commander can initiate upgrades.

Back

The Commander can initiate upgrades when a working Comm Center exist.
If all CCs are destroyed while an upgrade is in progress the upgrade is aborted and the money lost.

Aircraft Radar
Red markers for enemy aircraft are shown on map if the enemy aircraft is above 50 m and within 4 km of a working CC.

Artillery Radar
This upgrade will make Comm Centers able to track projectiles and put a small red cross at an estimated position of the artillery.

Sat Cam
Satellite camera which can track friendly units or be manually moved.
